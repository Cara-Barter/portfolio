About Me

I am an Indigenous woman who has a deep passion for the health of Mother Earth and helping others. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Indigenous Studies and Environmental Studies from the University of Victoria. I am a Fibre Artist turned Web Dev Weaver. I love solving puzzles to make products more usable. I employ my gifts of empathy and creativivty to reduce pain points for users and customers. I strive to really listen and understand people to understand their perspective and know what they are looking for.
